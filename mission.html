Our Mission

Our mission here at Root and Anchor Media Solutions is to foster creativity, diversity, reliability, and sustainability through the distribution and providance of technical support for multimedia projects ranging from webcomics to podcasts, as well as developing sites for our project partners.


Our Core Values

  • Creativity - We encourage clients to display their products in an imaginative fashion, and provide them with the means to do so.
  • Diversity - We strive to create a work environment and projects that display numerous backgrounds, beliefs and cultures, races, genders, and capabilites.
  • Reliability - We aim to provide support in a consistent and timely manner to our clients and partners.
  • Sustainability - We provide our clients with sites that are easily maintainable and flexible.
